How safe is your website?

freelancecentral.com is an IRS authorized e-file service provider. We’re an Electronic Return Originator or ERO that transmits tax return information directly to the IRS. Our software is tested by the IRS to ensure that every program is operating in a secure and proper way.

How will I know that the IRS has received my return?

freelancecentral.com is very transparent when it comes to the progress of your form 1099-NEC filings. We ensure that you are updated by following the below steps. After the IRS accepts your return, you will receive an email notification. You will also have access to an electronic version of the printable PDF of 1099-NEC form from the IRS. You will receive regular updates on your registered email address citing the process to complete your form 1099-NEC filing with the IRS

Generic Filing Queries

Why Employer Identification Number (EIN) is required to e-file IRS Form 1099-NEC?

IRS uses EIN to identify and protect your privacy. A combination of your EIN and name is used by the IRS as a unique ID. If your unique ID does not match the IRS records, then the filing would be rejected by the IRS.

Errors and Rejection

How to retrieve my password?

You can reset your password by clicking on ‘forgot password’ under the password field on the login page. You will be asked to enter your registered email address. Once you’ve entered the email address, click on ‘Reset Password’ A password reset link will be sent to your registered email address Open the email and click on the URL You will be prompted to enter your new password Confirm your password by reentering it Click on ‘Change My Password’ You will receive a message confirming the updation of your password.

Learn more...

After the electronic submission of Form 1099-NEC, I received an online duplicate filing error. Why did this happen?

This happened because the system detected that you had already filed the same form for the same filing year. Consider the below points to ensure this error does not happen again The rejection was due to return under the same EIN, Duplicate filing happened for the same tax period and the same recipient(s) We suggest you re-check your return to ensure that you are reporting the new 1099-NEC payments only and all the other information provided is correct.
