What is form 1099-nec?

It’s a form used for reporting independent contractor payments. Nec stands for non employee compensation.

Who needs to file form 1099-nec?

Filing form 1099-nec is mandatory for employers paying more than or equal to $600/anum to contract employees or consultants.

How to file form 1099-nec?

In order to file form 1099-nec to the irs, first you have to request form w-9 from your independent contractor. The 1099 filing deadline is 31st January.
